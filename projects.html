The Guild
Of Angels

Est. Paris

Currently, we are working on a sub-project, a social platform, a
for-profit charity, called "The Angels Guild" - La guilde des anges.
 With the aim of helping vulnerable groups of people and beginners
in entrepreneurship, through our unique micro-franchise business
models.

Including but not limited to: Business start-up assistance, website
development, graphic designs, marketing, branding services, business
consulting, various courses, collaborations, networking, etc
That our protégés can become independent and successful
entrepreneurs and support themselves and their families,and
ultimately, be able to contribute to their personal development,
education, mental and physical well-being.


One of our projects that is close to our hearts and in line with
our values
is the idea of an association, whose volunteers will give courses
on etiquette and good manners in schools. - We strongly believe
that good manners and knowledge of etiquette can have a beneficial
effect on children and their relationships with others, helping them
to build strong relationships throughout life, and even providing
more opportunities and success in life, as shown by research
conducted by psychologists in this field.


In addition, this program will focus on respectful and tolerant
behavior towards classmates and other people in general in order
to influence the reduction of aggression, harassment and
bullying in schools and beyond.

Satisfying interactions with the people around us make us happier
and affect our mental health.


As soon as we achieve a balance between our desires and our possibilities,
we will bring this idea to life.
If you have any ideas or a desire to realize such a project with us simply
contact us, we will be happy to hear from you.
